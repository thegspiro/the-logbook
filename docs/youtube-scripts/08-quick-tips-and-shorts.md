## Production Notes for Shorts

### Format Guidelines

- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical) for YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels / TikTok.
  Also render a 16:9 (horizontal) version for regular YouTube uploads.
- **Text Overlays:** Use large, readable text for key steps. Viewers on mobile
  may have sound off.
- **Captions:** Always include burned-in captions — many viewers watch without
  sound.
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts. No pauses longer than 1 second. Keep energy high but
  not frantic.
- **Branding:** Include "The Logbook" text logo in the corner throughout.
- **End Frame:** "Try The Logbook — Free & Open Source" with GitHub URL for
  2 seconds.

### Publishing Strategy

- **Release Schedule:** One short per week, published on Tuesdays or Thursdays
  (highest engagement for educational content).
- **Playlist:** Group all shorts into a "Quick Tips" playlist.
- **Cross-Promotion:** Each short should reference the full-length role guide
  in its description for viewers who want more depth.
- **Hashtags:** #FireDepartment #VolunteerFire #FirefighterLife #OpenSource
  #FireDeptTech
